Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine whether a product is counterfeit?

We consider pricing, condition, reviews, and direct inquiries. When uncertain, we buy and verify through official brand offices.

Do you pursue civil lawsuits in addition to criminal charges?

Yes. Civil suits offer faster resolution and penalties. Korean police deprioritize IP crimes, and criminal sanctions alone are often too weak.

What types of clients do you serve?

We represent global companies from the US, Canada, Japan, Sweden, Denmark, Italy, Switzerland, and more.

Are you using AI in your investigations?

We are preparing for AI integration but currently rely on trained staff for investigation and evidence collection.

What are some common tactics used by counterfeiters?

They mimic real prices and hide logos. Detection requires legal and investigative expertise.

How do you ensure clients are not financially burdened?

We cover litigation costs and only claim fees from counterfeiters. Clients owe nothing unless we win compensation.
