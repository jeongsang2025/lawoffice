Copyright Defense

1. Our Copyright Expertise

  • 25+ years of legal experience, including major cases for software IP (Microsoft, Adobe, Autodesk).
  • Strong foundation in digital IP, online piracy, and unauthorized image/use protection.

2. Practical Enforcement Tactics

  • Evidence collection through screenshots, consumer disguise, mystery shopping.
  • Verification of purchased goods via local office or global HQ.
  • Three-step response system: warning → negotiation → litigation.

3. Civil Litigation as Primary Strategy

  • Korean police deprioritize IP cases; criminal sanctions are minimal.
  • Civil suits provide quicker resolution and monetary penalties.
  • Case-specific judgment on whether to use warnings, negotiation, civil/criminal suits.

4. Trained IP Staff

  • Investigators act as mystery buyers to collect detailed evidence.
  • Most IP enforcement depends on non-attorney staff execution.
  • Our internal team is highly experienced and thoroughly trained.

5. Common Scenarios Addressed

  • Fake product listings that mimic real pricing.
  • Listings without brand names/logos but intended to mislead consumers.
  • AI-assisted monitoring under consideration — not yet a substitute for human intel.

6. Global Client Collaboration

  • We bridge language, legal, and strategy gaps between local Korean courts and global HQs.
  • Participation in IP forums (e.g., INTA) for direct client interaction and trend updates.
